Hey There! I’m Jun, a UX/UI designer who crossed over to UX design from the hospitality industry. From coming up with solutions for guests’ needs and concerns, to creating exceptional experiences for all of our hotel guests - I was able to practice and hone in on my problem solving and empathy skills on a daily basis.

As a UX/UI designer, I’m able to transfer those problem solving skills to my research and ideation phase by designing an enjoyable user experience by putting myself in the shoe’s of the users. What intrigues me about design is that it is ever changing and constantly evolving to be better than what it was before .

Apart from the user experience, I am passionate about traveling and discovering a city through the people and food. I also enjoy cooking recipes found on YouTube and making my friends test them with me.
